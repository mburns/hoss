The Humanitarian Free and Open Source Software (HFOSS) movement is dedicated to forming better communities. By creating, sharing, and hosting altruistic software, the various HFOSS programs strive to improve the world through digital volunteering.

The Enable project is designed to help people with disabilities. By offering our software on mobile devices, we aim to make HFOSS portable and pertinent in everyday life.

Please search through our Find Software page for list of current software. If you can't find what you need, please use our Suggest a Project page to suggest your desired project and get in touch with volunteer programmers.

To find out more about the projects that our volunteer programmers are working on, please check Seach Projects page.

If you'd like to help, please check our Contribute page. Of course, donations are always welcome.
